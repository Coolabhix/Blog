Bristle Bots

  • Author: Abhinav
  • Tagged: Bengaluru,India
  • Created: 20th May, 2022

Ahh Bristle Bots!
What are they? Well A bristlebot or brushbot is an extremely simple form of walking robot. It is one of the simplest of all mobile robots, both in its function and its construction. As a result of this ease of construction, they have become popular projects at the school science fair level. They dont require any maintainence and eat very little food(batteries).

Components

Ive made many types of Bristle Bots using a shoe brush, a cloth washing brush and some toothbrush heads. All you need to make a bristleBot is a toothbrush head, a vibrating motor, and a battery
The total cost of making a bristle bot is about 20rs. But having a bristle bot is priceless!

Table Lamp - Fan - Bluetooth Speaker - Pen Stand

  • Author: Abhinav
  • Tagged: Bengaluru,India
  • Created: 18th May, 2022

Woah, That's a lot of stuff!
"Hmm I dont have a table lamp... and its pretty expensive to buy one. so lets just make one!"
Thats where I got the idea from. A table lamp, a fan which can be powered through a phone , a laptop or a power bank( Dont want to use your device's battery? Well I've added a 9v battery for backup too! ), A pen stand and a bluetooth speaker. What else can you want?

Components

Components I used : An old Popcorn box as the pen stand, A selfie stick acting as the lamp stand, two big white LEDs, a 9v Battery, A toggle switch for the lamp, a Normal switch for the fan, A 9v DC motor(Thank you Sonal didi) and an old usb cable cut and attached to the DC motor for supporting various devices.

Buzz Wire Game

  • Author: Abhinav
  • Tagged: Bengaluru,India
  • Created: 18th May, 2022

This game is very Easy to make and is a lot of fun!
I've made it from scrap material lying around in my house.
In this game you guide your hooked wire through this maze-wire and you have to do it very carefully as if the hook touches the wire the buzzer will Make sound and a Red light will flash indicating "Game over". I've also added some Electricl tape as a Checkpoint. If you touch the wire, you have to start again.

Components

Components I used: A wooden piece as the base, A Clothes hanger as the maze, a 9v Battery, a Red LED, a 5v Buzzer(Thank you Sonal didi), and a switch.
